Love Connections was created in 2018 with the intention of helping LGBTQ+ youth. Many LGBTQ+ youth struggle with their identity. This website was created to help the struggling youth come to terms with who they are and feel comfortable with themsleves. We provide information for those who want to support members of the community but aren't part of it themsleves. We also provides external resources for conflicts that go beyond figuring out and accepting one's identity.
